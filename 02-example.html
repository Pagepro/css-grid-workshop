Hints

Creating grid:

  • display: grid;

Grid gap:

  • grid-gap: gap-size;
  • grid-column-gap: column-gap-size;
  • grid-row-gap: row-gap-size;

Creating rows and columns:

  • grid-template-columns: track-size;
  • grid-template-rows: track-size;
  • grid-template: grid-template-rows/grid-template-columns;
  • grid-auto-columns: tracks-size;
  • grid-auto-rows: tracks-size;

Functions:

  • grid-template-columns: repeat(how-many, size);
  • grid-template-columns: repeat(how-many, minmax(min-value, max-value));
  • grid-template-columns: repeat(auto-fill, minmax(min-value, max-value));
